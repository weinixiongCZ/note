# Polynomial equation
## Cubic equation in one element
### An example
$$
\begin{gather*}
a+b+c=1\\
a^{2} +b^{2} +c^{2} =2\\
a^{3} +b^{3} +c^{3} =3\\
\\
ab+bc+ac=-\frac{1}{2}\\
x^{3} -x^{2} -\frac{1}{2} x-\frac{1}{2} =0
\end{gather*}
$$
let $\displaystyle f_{n} =a^{n} +b^{n} +c^{n}$，we get $\displaystyle f_{n} =f_{n-1} +\frac{1}{2} f_{n-2} +\frac{1}{2} f_{n-3}$，$\displaystyle n >3$
